About

Dr. Chia-Hsuan Chang is a postdoctoral associate in the Clinical NLP lab, led by Dr. Hua Xu, at the Department of Biomedical Informatics and Data Science at Yale School of Medicine. His research focuses on natural language processing (NLP) and large language models (LLMs), particularly their applications in enhancing healthcare and literature-based discovery. Additionally, he is interested in developing efficient algorithms for topic identification in texts and conducting cross-lingual text analysis.

He obtained a Ph.D. in Information Systems from National Sun Yat-sen University, under the advice of Dr. San-Yih Hwang. Previously, he was a postdoctoral researcher in the College of Computing & Informatics at Drexel University, mentored by Dr. Christopher Yang.
