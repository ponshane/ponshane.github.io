I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Health Informatics Research Lab at Drexel University College of Computing & Informatics. My research interests include data mining, text mining, and health informatics, focusing on using data to solve problems in the healthcare area. I received my Ph.D. in information management from the National Sun Yat-sen University in July 2021, advised by Dr. San-Yih Hwang. I was a visiting student in the college of computing & informatics at Drexel University from February 2020 to January 2021.
